About Me

As a graduate of Astronomy and a dedicated cybersecurity professional, I bring a unique analytical perspective to the world of digital defense. My background in space science has equipped me with a deep understanding of data-driven modeling and the critical importance of robust infrastructure, both terrestrial and in orbit.

My professional focus is on offensive and defensive security (Purple Teaming), where I apply a scientific methodology to penetration testing and Security Operations Center (SOC) analysis. I am passionate about translating complex scientific models, like the Ionospheric Bubble Probability (IBP) model, into functional, high-performance code and using modern tools to visualize and interpret the results.
